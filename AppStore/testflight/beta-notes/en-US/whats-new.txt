This update makes RetroRapid! more stable and polished.

We fixed a crash that could occur when sound effects or haptic cues were playing, aligned in-app branding so menus and settings show RetroRapid! correctly throughout, and achievement screens now show the real achievement artwork instead of placeholders.

Game Center achievements, friend markers, and sharing are unchanged — still the best way to chase your crew.

What we'd like you to focus on:
- Play several runs with sound and haptic lane cues enabled
- Open Game Center achievements and confirm artwork looks correct
- Check Settings and About for consistent RetroRapid! branding
- Report any unexpected crashes, especially around game-over or lane changes

Thanks for racing with us.
