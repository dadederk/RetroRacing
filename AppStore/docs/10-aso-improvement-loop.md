## Next ASO Improvement Loop

After this metadata pass, the next improvement should be measurement rather than more keyword churn:

1. Capture the pre-change baseline in App Store Connect Analytics and Appfigures/Krankie: product page views, conversion rate, App Store Search downloads, and tracked keyword ranks.
2. Validate the provisional keyword fields against current ranks, suggested keywords, and competitor terms. Replace weak terms before submission.
3. Resolve the shared App Info lock, then apply and verify the recommended name/subtitle localizations.
4. Resolve visionOS availability or keep all Apple Vision claims out of the metadata.
5. Regenerate screenshot exports from Screenshot Studio so rendered images match the revised source copy and recommended slide order.
6. Submit one coherent metadata pack for the currently supported localizations. Keep `es-MX` in the pack only after its field and storefront behavior are confirmed in App Store Connect.
7. Re-check ranks after 7, 14, and 28 days, separating brand/noisy terms from intent-matched terms such as retro racing, traffic racer, watch game, reflex game, VoiceOver game, and localized arcade/traffic phrases.
8. If traffic is high enough, use Product Page Optimization to test screenshot-first variants rather than changing metadata repeatedly.
9. Create Custom Product Pages only for clear segments: accessibility/VoiceOver, Apple Watch, controller/Mac play, and Spanish/Catalan storefronts.
10. Keep release notes in the established RetroRapid voice and use feature updates to support keyword themes without stuffing metadata.
